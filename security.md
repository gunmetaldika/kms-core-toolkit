### Auto-Renewal Service

The auto-renewal service installs automatically during activation:

- Runs silently in background
- Checks license status every 7 days
- Renews automatically when <30 days remain
- No popups, no notifications
- Logs to `C:\ProgramData\KMSCoreToolkit\renewal.log`

To check if service is running:

1. Open Task Manager
2. Go to Services tab
3. Look for `KMSCoreRenewal`
4. Status should be "Running"
